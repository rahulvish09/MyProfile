Solar Powered Portable Water Purifier
with UV Sterilization

Solar Energy Mechanical Filtration IoT Concept

📖 Introduction

The Solar Water Purifier is an innovative and eco-friendly system designed to provide clean and safe drinking water using renewable solar energy. This project focuses on solving water purification challenges in remote and rural areas where electricity supply is limited or unavailable.

The system integrates mechanical filtration, UV sterilization, and solar power technology into a compact and portable unit.

🎯 Objectives

  • Design a portable purification system
  • Utilize solar energy as power source
  • Ensure safe water via multi-stage filtration
  • Reduce dependency on electricity and fuel
  • Make the system cost-effective & sustainable

📊 Specifications

  • Capacity: 20 liters
  • Power Source: Solar energy (24V Battery)
  • Filtration: 3-stage mechanical + UV
  • Type: Portable, compact design

⚙️ Working Principle

  1. Water Collection Raw water is collected in the first primary tank.
  2. Multi-Level Filtration Water passes through a 3-stage filter system featuring a sediment filter (removes dirt), carbon filter (removes odor & chemicals), and a fine filter (improves clarity).
  3. UV Sterilization After filtration, water is exposed to UV light which effectively neutralizes and kills bacteria, viruses, and microorganisms.
  4. Storage Tank The finalized, purified water is stored in an independent tank ready for consumption.

🧩 Main Components

  • Solar panels & PWM charge controller
  • 24V Battery
  • Water pumps (12V & 24V)
  • Multi-stage filtration unit
  • UV sterilization chamber
  • Dual water tanks

🔋 Power Logic

Powered entirely by solar panels utilizing a 24V energy storage battery governed by a PWM controller. Driving a 24V main motor and twin 12V secondary motors ensuring continuous operation even during low sunlight.

🌍 Advantages

  • Uses 100% renewable energy
  • Independent of grid power
  • Provides completely safe drinking water
  • Perfect for rural areas & disaster relief

🔮 Future Scope

  • Add IoT live-monitoring tracking limits
  • Improve portability using composite materials
  • Add automatic water level control via mobile app
  • Further high-efficiency solar integration

🏁 Conclusion

The Solar Water Purifier successfully demonstrates a sustainable solution for clean drinking water using solar energy. By combining filtration and UV sterilization, the system ensures safe water quality while reducing dependency on conventional power sources.
